Prabhas


Prabhas, born as Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju, is a popular Indian actor known primarily for his work in Telugu cinema. He gained nationwide fame for his role as the protagonist in the epic *Baahubali* series, directed by S. S. Rajamouli. His portrayal of Amarendra Baahubali catapulted him to immense popularity, making him one of the highest-paid actors in India. Known for his dedication to roles and humble demeanor, Prabhas has continued to expand his career with pan-Indian releases like *Saaho* and *Radhe Shyam*. His fans appreciate his versatility, hard work, and down-to-earth personality.

Dulquer Salman


Dulquer Salmaan is a popular Indian actor and producer known for his work primarily in Malayalam cinema, as well as in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi films. The son of legendary actor Mammootty, Dulquer has carved his own path with versatile roles and natural charisma. He made his debut in *Second Show* (2012) and gained recognition with films like *Ustad Hotel*, *Charlie*, *Bangalore Days*, and *Mahanati*. Dulquer is known for choosing diverse and challenging roles, and his performances have earned him a large fan base across India. His down-to-earth nature and charm make him one of the most beloved actors of his generation.
